王德生是SDE（结构-差异-纠缠）思想体系的创始人，一位独立运营的"超级个体"研究者与平台开发者。其学术背景源于计算数学，经由Voronoi/Delaunay剖分与有限元网格生成的深层研究，逐步演化出一套跨域知识发生学方法论——SDE。

Wang Desheng is the founder of the SDE (Structure–Difference–Entanglement) thought system, an independent "super-individual" researcher and platform developer. His academic background in computational mathematics — deep work on Voronoi/Delaunay triangulation and finite element mesh generation — evolved into a cross-domain knowledge-genesis methodology: SDE.

SDE的核心命题是：新知识结构在特定纠缠（E）条件下，经由差异流（D）的驱动而发生（S）。这一公式不是隐喻，而是跨越艺术、治理、教育、企业、哲学、宗教、数学等二十余个领域的实际操作框架。

SDE's core proposition: new knowledge structures emerge (S) through the drive of difference-flow (D) under specific entanglement (E) conditions. This formula is not a metaphor but an operational framework spanning over twenty domains — from art and governance to mathematics and AI.

迄今已出版57部专著（约每部20万字，均在Bowker ISBN注册），目标100部。每一部都是SDE在该领域的实战展开，共同构成一部跨域知识发生学的百科全书。

To date, 57 monographs have been published (~200,000 characters each, all ISBN-registered on Bowker), with a target of 100. Each is a live deployment of SDE in a specific domain, together forming a cross-domain encyclopedia of knowledge genesis.

结构 · 差异 · 纠缠

Structure · Difference · Entanglement

SDE不是框架，而是知识的发生机制。它解释新知识结构在什么条件下生长出来。

SDE is not a framework but a generative mechanism of knowledge — explaining the conditions under which new knowledge structures come into being.

S
结构
Structure

新知识的显现形态。结构不是给定的，而是在特定纠缠与差异流的驱动下生长出来的。S是发生的终态，也是下一轮发生的起点。

The emergent form of new knowledge. Structure is not given but grown — driven by specific entanglement and difference-flow. S is the end-state of one genesis and the starting point of the next.

D
差异
Difference

驱动结构发生的动力源。差异不是简单的不同，而是具有方向性的差异流——正是它的流动方式决定了新结构的形态与路径。

The motive force driving structural genesis. Difference is not mere distinction but directional difference-flow — its mode of movement determines the form and pathway of new structure.

E
纠缠
Entanglement

发生的场域条件。纠缠是元素之间深层的相互作用网络——它决定哪些差异流能被激活，哪些结构发生具有可能性。

The field condition of genesis. Entanglement is the deep network of interaction among elements — it determines which difference-flows can be activated and which structural emergences are possible.

核心公式 · Core Formula

S = H(D, E)  ·  D = G(S, E)  ·  E = F(S, D)
